## 6. Your Privacy Rights Under Australian Law

### 6.1 Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) Rights
Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Commonwealth), you have the following rights:
- **Access (APP 12):** Request access to your personal information we hold
- **Correction (APP 13):** Request correction of inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete, irrelevant, or misleading personal information
- **Complaints:** Make a complaint about our handling of your personal information
- **Anonymity and Pseudonymity (APP 2):** Where practicable, interact with us anonymously or using a pseudonym

### 6.2 Western Australian Rights
Once the Privacy and Responsible Information Sharing Act 2024 (WA) commences (expected 2026), additional rights may apply to interactions with WA public sector agencies.

### 6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise your privacy rights:

**Email:** info@nexamap.com.au

**Response Times:**
- We will acknowledge your request within 14 days
- We will respond to access requests within 30 days (may be extended in complex cases)
